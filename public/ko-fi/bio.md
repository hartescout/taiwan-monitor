## Recommended Medium Bio

Pharos is an open-source conflict intelligence dashboard built to track the Iran conflict in real time. It combines events, actor responses, live map stories, signals, and daily briefs into one product so users can understand the full picture of a conflict, not just fragments.

Support helps cover AI usage, hosting, database, map, and infrastructure costs so the project can stay online and keep improving. This is not for profit. All support goes to operating costs.
