# Cover Image Guidance

## Recommended Direction

Use the dashboard map screenshot as the Ko-fi cover image.

Base asset:
- `public/ko-fi/cover-base.png`

## Why

- it immediately shows what Pharos actually is
- the map, timelines, and overlays communicate the product better than a generic banner
- it feels grounded in the real interface rather than promotional art

## Composition Guidance

- favor a crop that keeps the live map and sidebar visible
- avoid placing important details too close to the top or far edges in case Ko-fi crops the image differently
- if text is added later, keep it minimal and high-contrast

## Suggested Cover Message (Optional)

If you add text on the image later, keep it short:
- Support the infrastructure behind Pharos
- Help keep Pharos online

Best default option:
- no text overlay, just a strong crop of the product itself
